About the Artist

Arden prides himself on telling a story through color, light, and brush stroke. His portraits invite you into the personality of the subject, his landscapes a nostalgic glimpse into the history of the place, his playful application of color challenges the viewer to look closer, stop longer, and seek solace in the subtle illusions of light and shadow in their own surroundings.

Inspired by Norman Rockwell and grandmother, Kathie Arden, this NY-based Cleveland ex-pat brings a midwestern flare to the North American art scene.
